Newfoundland Dog Breed

        Newfoundland dogs were originally working dogs in Newfoundland used to pull nets for fishermen or drag wood. These dogs are extremely well swimmers and their sweet and lovable personality makes for a great family pet.

        Newfoundlands are a giant breed weighing around 100 to 150 pounds once they are full-grown. However, a female typically ranges from around 100-120 pounds while a male weighs around 130-150 pounds. Although being a giant fluffy fur-ball doesn't sound bad, their size contributes to many serious orthopedic problems and causes them to have a shorter lifespan of around 9-10 years.

        A love to swim and be outdoors doesn't make Newfoundlands any less of a loyal and loving companion. Newfoundlands are eager to please and are highly trainable dogs. Whether you decide on a Newfoundland for their size, fluffiness, agility, or trainability, they are sure to make the perfect pet!
